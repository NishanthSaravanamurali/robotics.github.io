The General Idea...

Proposed Method:

   We are going to use a 4 DOF delta type parallel robot which helps in product assembly lines. The robot has four degrees of freedom, which means it can move in four independent directions or axes. These degrees of freedom are typically classified into three translational (Prismatic) and one rotational (Rotatory) motions. The delta robot consists of a fixed base with three parallel arms connected to a moving platform (end-effector). The arms are typically arranged in a triangular or delta configuration, hence the name. The delta robot's design employs a parallel structure, meaning that the end-effector is connected to the base through multiple parallel kinematic chains. This configuration provides advantages such as high rigidity, accuracy, and speed.

Novel Implementation:

   It is lacking a DOF and thus in this project we are increasing the DOF by 1 by adding a revolute joint to the end effector and thereby increasing its functionalities.
